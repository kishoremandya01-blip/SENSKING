Quick Headshot Tips

01

Aim at neck level

Always position your crosshair at neck height before shooting. Gun recoil naturally pulls bullets upward — straight to the head.

02

Use burst fire

Don't hold the trigger. Tap 2–3 times quickly. This gives your crosshair time to reset between shots for consistent headshots.

03

Lower scope sensitivity

Your scope sensitivity should always be lower than your general sensitivity. This gives you precise aim when zoomed in.

04

Practice in training mode

Spend 10 minutes in training mode every day with your new settings before going ranked. Muscle memory takes 3–5 days to build.
